Frequently asked questions

How do I know which service is right for me? +

It depends on where you are in your product journey. Quick UX Wins if you need fast insights, Redesign Packages if you're ready for a full overhaul, and Ongoing Support if you want continuous guidance. I'm happy to chat about the best fit.

How does payment work? +

For projects, I typically ask for an initial deposit (usually 30-50%) upon approval of the Scope of Work. The remainder is due upon delivery. For retainers, payment is typically monthly or as agreed.

I have a limited budget‚Äîcan I still work with you? +

Absolutely. I offer Quick UX Wins packages starting at $2.5k, and I can customize the scope to fit your budget. Let's chat about what's possible within your constraints.

Why does UX cost this much? +

Good UX is an investment that drives business results. The cost includes strategy, research, design, testing, and iteration. You're not just paying for pretty screens‚Äîyou're paying for solutions that increase conversions, retention, and user satisfaction.

What's the timeline for a typical project? +

Quick UX Wins: 1-2 weeks. Redesign Packages: 3-4 months. Depends on scope and complexity. I always prioritize quality over rushing.

Do you do branding or coding? +

My focus is UX/product design and strategy. I work closely with developers and brand teams, but I don't do branding or development work directly. I can recommend trusted partners if needed.
